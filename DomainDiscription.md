Every senior project group must build and publish a public website about their project.
To complete the website, you will need the following components:
1. Domain Name
Your project’s unique web address
(Example: www.smartparking-system.com)
2. Hosting
Where your website files and pages are stored so visitors can view them.
3. Professional Email Address
An email that uses your domain name
(Example: info@smartparking-system.com)
4. Your Website (developed by your team)
The actual content, pages, design, and project information.
Choosing Your Domain Name
Your domain should:
Be related to your project title
Look professional
Be short, memorable, and easy to spell
Avoid numbers, extra symbols, or slang
Service Providers
Each component can come from a different provider.
You may buy your domain from one company, host the website on another, and use email services from a third.
You are responsible for linking and integrating them correctly.
Examples:
Buy the domain from NameCheap
Host the website on GitHub Pages
Use email hosting from NameCheap, BlueHost, or Hostinger
